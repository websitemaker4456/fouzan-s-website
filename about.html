Biography

Fouzan Tanveer is a pioneer in AI-driven sales automation, specializing in lead generation, enrichment, and nurturing workflows.

He is the founder of GrowZilla, a cutting-edge automation agency, and has architected scalable solutions using platforms like Clay, Make.com, Retell AI, and VAPI to drive qualified leads for B2B SaaS and service businesses.

Fouzan has automated thousands of outreach workflows combining enriched contact scraping, personalized cold emails, and AI voice agents. His systems have collectively influenced millions in pipeline revenue, helping startups and agencies reduce manual prospecting efforts by up to 80%. He's also contributed to the development of multi-channel lead booking ecosystems with robust reporting infrastructures.

He has been trusted to consult and build automation for venture-backed startups, lean agencies, and growth-stage founders across the globe. Currently, he is focused on scaling intelligent agents that can autonomously identify, qualify, and convert prospects into customers.
